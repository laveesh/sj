Session Chair, AMLTA-2020, Manipal University, Jaipur, India

The 5th International Conference on Advanced Machine Learning Technologies and Applications (AMLTA-2020) was organized by the Manipal University, Jaipur, on February 13th-15th, 2020 to learn more about the emergence of the technologies in the fields of Real-time problems and their respective mitigations. Dr. Sunil Kumar Jangir, Assistant Professor, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Mody University of Science and Technology, Lakshmangarh took out some time from his busy schedule to Chair the Oral Presentations Session held at the conference. The new insights of various researches were highlighted by the divine talk of some of the experienced folks which included Prof. Nitin Tripathi, Asian Institute of Technology, Thailand, Prof. Shalabh Gupta, IISC, Banglore, Mr. Shubham Gupta, Dell Technologies, Banglore, Prof. Aboul Ella Jassanien, Chair-SRGE and Prof. Hamidah Ibrahim. This conference turned out to be a platform to grasp the knowledge and enhance the intelligence.

Session Chair, ICTCS-2019, Bhupal Nobels' University, Udaipur, India

The 4th International Conference on Information and Communication Technology for Competitive Strategies (ICTCS-2019) organized by the BHUPAL NOBLES' UNIVERSITY, UDAIPUR, India , held on December 13th-14th, 2019 provided a platform to learn about new and emerging technologies. Dr. Sunil Kumar Jangir, Assistant Professor, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Mody University of Science and Technology, Lakshmangarh was invited as a part of this conference as the member of the session chair for the evaluation of the research papers that foreground the usage of information systems, distributed AI, intelligent agents, and collaborative work, to explore and discuss various aspects of design and development of intelligent technologies. The conference was devoted to increase the understanding role of Information and Communication Technology, how Intelligent Systems has day by day evolved. The conference gave a platform for bringing forth significant research and literature across the field of Information and Communication Technology for Intelligent Systems and provide an overview of the technologies waiting unveiling.
